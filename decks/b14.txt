Dā mihi = “Give me a drachm of silver,” said a Cynic philosopher to Antigonus. “That is not a present befitting a king,” replied he. “Give me then a talent,” said the other. “That is not a present befitting a Cynic.” —Italian Proverb

Give us = Dā nōbīs
Give them = Dā eīs
Give me (s) = Dā mihi
Give me (p) = Dāte mihi
